Price to Value Discrepancy “I hope what you’re giving them is worth it.” Those words would probably sting for most, but when my dad said them to me, I just knew h e didn’t understand the value we were providing. What I want to show you is how to create and communicate value, aka th e “worth-it-ness” of an offer. In order to understand how to make a compelling offer, you must understand value. The reaso n people buy anything is to get a deal. They believe what they are getting (VALUE) is worth more than what they are giv ing in exchange for it (PRICE). The moment the value they receive dips below what they are paying, they stop buyin g from you. This price to value discrepancy is what you need to avoid at all costs. After all, as Warren Buffet said, “Price is what you pay. Value is what you get.”
